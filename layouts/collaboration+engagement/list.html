Collaboration is a major part of my practice and I’ve typically worked long-term with a few people, building up a way of working together and blending different techniques. My work is also rooted in engagement. I’ve run workshops with school children, LGBTQ+ elders, people who have experienced head trauma, neurodivergent young adults and various other groups and scenarios. My preferred way of working is long-form interaction where the final outcome is a co-created artwork. I’m interested in this process being the core part of the artwork rather than an additional add-on.
